## Design Context

### Users
Students and evaluators in an academic lab/demo context, especially for invigilator review. They need to quickly understand compiler-stage outputs and prove correctness under time pressure.

### Brand Personality
Mature, confident, technically expert.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean and professional technical UI with restrained visual language, strong hierarchy, and purposeful interactions. Avoid novelty-heavy styling and avoid decorative noise.

### Quality Bar
MVP polish.

### Design Principles
1. Clarity first: every panel should communicate state and output at a glance.
2. Consistency over novelty: shared tokens and components for repeated interactions.
3. Trustworthy feedback: clear compile/copy success and error messaging.
4. Accessible motion: smooth transitions with reduced-motion support.
5. Reviewer-ready presentation: polished spacing, alignment, and icon consistency.
